# Waggin-tag'em Proposal

## User Story

AS A dog owner, I WANT a doggy play-mate for my own dog, SO THAT their social needs can be met.

## Acceptance Criteria

```md
GIVEN a dog meet-up site

WHEN I visit the site for the first time
THEN I am presented with the homepage, option to signup for an account or login

WHEN I click on the signup link
THEN I see a signup page and prompted button
THEN my user credentials are saved and I am logged into the site

WHEN I click on the login link
THEN I see a modal prompt for username and password
WHEN I click on the login button
THEN I am taken to the user profile page with sidbar navigation options to: add a pet, view my pets, view saved pup friends, chats, or logout

WHEN I click on the add a pet option
THEN I am presented with a form to fill in pet information
WHEN I click on the add pet button
THEN the pet profile is saved as a card under the my account

WHEN I click on the view my pets option
THEN I am presented with all the pet profiles saved under my account with options to update or delete

WHEN I click on the view saved pup friends option
THEN I am presented with all the pet profile friends I have saved

WHEN I click on the chats option
THEN I am presented with any chats I have between other users

WHEN I click on the logout option in the navigation
THEN I am signed out of the site
```
